034:  * Offset       Register

036:  * 0x004        SWM_PINASSIGN1          Pin assign register 1
037:  * 0x008        SWM_PINASSIGN2          Pin assign register 2
038:  * 0x00c        SWM_PINASSIGN3          Pin assign register 3
039:  * 0x010        SWM_PINASSIGN4          Pin assign register 4
040:  * 0x014        SWM_PINASSIGN5          Pin assign register 5
041:  * 0x018        SWM_PINASSIGN6          Pin assign register 6
042:  * 0x01c        SWM_PINASSIGN7          Pin assign register 7
043:  * 0x020        SWM_PINASSIGN8          Pin assign register 8
044:  * 0x1c0        SWM_PINENABLE0          Pin enable register 0
045:  */
